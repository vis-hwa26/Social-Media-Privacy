PROTECT YOUR PRIVACY ONLINE



What is the need of Social media privacy?



Personal Autonomy and Control:

Users value the ability to control who sees their personal information, such as contact details, location, and personal photos. Privacy settings empower users to decide how much of their life is shared publicly.




Safety and Security:

With increasing incidents of cyberstalking, identity theft, and financial fraud originating from information gleaned on social media, users are rightfully concerned about their safety and seek to limit the accessibility of their personal data.



Professional Reputation:

The pressure to maintain a particular image on social media can be mentally exhausting. Privacy controls allow users to navigate these spaces with less pressure, knowing they have control over their audience.

Social Media Security Checklist

Manage Privacy Settings

These settings allow you to make sure you are only sharing with friends and family. These setting can change with updates so you should check them regularly.

Dont Enable Auto Login

If someone gets access to your phone or computer make sure they can’t automatically access your social sites. Make sure your computer’s browser “remember” your login and password isn’t set.

Use Two-factor authentication 2FA

‘double checking’ you really are who you say you are by texting your phone a code that you need to enter before you can log in.

Setup Security Answers

You can set up security questions on your accounts use questions that are hard to find out about you.
